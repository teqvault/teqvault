Roadmap

From Prototype to Platforms

Four phases. One direction. Every milestone is designed to compound on the last — each product we ship makes the next one faster to build and harder to ignore.

Phase 01 — Now

Foundation

Launch and grow Quetiemals on global mobile app stores. Build an active player base, establish live-ops content cadence, and validate core match-3 RPG mechanics through real user data. Simultaneously publish the Eyuforyia literary IP to anchor the transmedia universe.

Phase 02 — Near Term

Synergy

Expand the IP library. Launch Aeterna Divisa, Boot Camp, DomeBound, and The Death Road as published works. Each manuscript becomes a cross-medium asset — feeding interactive e-books, audio experiences, and future game adaptations from day one.

Phase 03 — Mid Term

Scaling

Port successful mobile IPs to PC and console with high-fidelity visual upgrades. Expand Quetiemals from mobile-first to a full multi-platform title. Partner with publishers and studios to accelerate the console pipeline and reach enthusiast gaming markets.

Integration

Achieve full vertical integration through proprietary hardware. Develop embedded tactical simulators, specialized physical interfaces, and custom electronics R&D designed to interact natively with our software ecosystems — completing the vision of a studio that controls every layer of the experience it delivers.
